About us

Balogh Wines is a Chilean company with international operations worldwide within the Wines industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce exactly what the market is looking for in terms of quality, price, story behind the wine, and packaging. Our wines have been selected through a deep evaluation performed by our winemakers. This selection process has the purpose of offering wines with special value and high potential for demanding customers, offering also Brand Exclusivity within their region.

Our Business Model

We work with two different business models:

  1. Direct Chilean Wine Export: We develop, produce and export our own brands and also customized wines according to the customers` requirements, this in terms of quality, price, and packaging, positionong, among others.

  2. Indirect Chilean Wine Representation: We have alliances with different partner wineries and also offer the representation of their brands to customers worldwide. These wineries are recognized because of the quality of their wines, their history and their international awards.

Our Team

Our team is composed by skilled winemakers with outstanding international experience in wine making and in the wine business and a dedicated customer service. Balogh Wines' people is committed to deliver the best of Chile to our customers worldwide.
