3.1. Footer Part

This Theme has widgetized footer.

Go to the Appearance > Widgets to find your available theme widgets.

Footer is widgetized. You can have one column, two columns or three columns widgetized footer. If you drag and drop one widget inside "Footer Widget Area" you'll have one column footer area. If you drag and drop two widgets inside "Footer Widget Area" you'll have two columns footer area and if you upload three widgets you'll have three columns footer area. You are able to drag and drop more than three widgets inside "Footer Widget Area" and they will show up bellow the three columns widget.
